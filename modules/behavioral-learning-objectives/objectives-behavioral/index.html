Objectives:

By the end of this module, the student teacher will be able to:

  • Define the concept of digital behavioral objectives.
  • Determine the components of a digital behavioral objective (behavior, condition, criterion).
  • Classify digital objectives according to Bloom’s taxonomy levels.
  • Formulate measurable digital behavioral objectives using appropriate digital tools.
  • Evaluate the behavioral objectives generated by artificial intelligence applications.
  • Appreciate the importance of formulating digital behavioral objectives in improving the quality of education.
  • Participate actively in online discussions about the importance of digital behavioral objectives.
  • Collaborate with peers in formulating digital behavioral objectives for lessons.
  • Respect peers’ opinions during discussions about digital learning objectives.
