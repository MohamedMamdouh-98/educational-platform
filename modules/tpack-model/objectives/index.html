Learning Objectives

At the end of this module, the student teacher should be able to:

  • Define the TPACK model.
  • Determine the dimensions of the TPACK model in the field of mathematics education.
  • Distinguish between:
    • Content knowledge
    • Pedagogical knowledge
    • Technological knowledge
  • Explain the importance of the TPACK model in improving the educational process.
  • Deduce the mathematical content knowledge.
  • Deduce the pedagogical knowledge in the teaching and learning of mathematics.
  • Deduce the technological knowledge in the teaching and learning of mathematics.
  • Analyze a mathematics teaching situation in light of the TPACK model.
