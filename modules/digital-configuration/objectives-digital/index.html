Objectives:

At the end of this module, the student teacher will be able to:

  • Define the concept of prerequisite knowledge in mathematics.
  • Explain the importance of identifying prerequisite knowledge before teaching a mathematics lesson.
  • Define the concept of digital lesson introduction in teaching mathematics.
  • Explain the importance of digital introduction in teaching mathematics.
  • Distinguish between different types of digital introductions suitable for various teaching situations.
  • Use digital tools such as Padlet, Mentimeter, and Google Forms to implement lesson introduction.
  • Employ digital media such as images or videos to present a lesson introduction.
  • Design digital introduction activities suitable for Mathematics lessons.
  • Appreciate the importance of digital tools in motivating learners.
  • Collaborate with peers to design digital introduction activities.
